## Overview

HCMS is an enterprise healthcare case management system used to manage and maintain patient case records throughout their lifecycle. It centralizes critical healthcare information, enabling users to review, update, and track case data efficiently.

## Features
- Patient case management
- Patient demographics and medical history
- Medication tracking
- Adverse reaction documentation
- Important clinical dates and timelines
- Document and attachment management
- Case review and reporting workflows

## Workflow
1. Load or create a patient case.
2. Review and update patient information.
3. Record medications, reactions, and important dates.
4. Validate case information.
5. Generate reports and complete the case lifecycle.
